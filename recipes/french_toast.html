French Toast


Home

Description

This easy French Toast recipe is the BEST, with a thicker batter and cinnamon sugar flavors that really set it apart.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 thick slices of bread

Steps

  1. Heat skillet over medium heat.
  2. Start by mixing the flour and egg together in a shallow dish, then whisk in the milk, sugar, salt, cinnamon, and vanilla.
  3. Dip the bread slices into the batter, making sure both sides are covered, and place them on the skillet.
  4. Cook them for a few minutes, until the bottom of the bread starts to get golden brown. Then flip and cook the other side.
  5. Plate and serve them warm with syrup and a sprinkle of powdered sugar
